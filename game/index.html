TAXI SIM 2D is a Simulation - Arcade game, a game about Taxi & Life as a Taxi Driver. You, as a player are supposed to search for fares (i.e. Pedestrains / People who want to go at their desired place), let them get into your taxi and drop them safely at their destination, provided that you don't crash into Traffic and reach / arrive late.

How to search for a fare?

Pedestrains / Fares - are obviously those who walk on Footpaths. I hope you understand how and what Footpaths look in the game. Fares are enclosed in a coloured circle. You have to drive up your taxi to / and touch the circle to receive information (i.e. Destination, Time, etc) of the Fare. If you're interested in going to that destination, press Y and drop the Fare at the destination.

Help! The destination has a negative sign!

Press Z to flip the canvas in order to drop the Fare. The taxi will be turned to the opposite direction, enabling you to drop faster instead of being a reverse king / queen.

Notes :

1. If the Distance has a negative sign , press Z to flip the Canvas.
2. You have to complete atleast 3 Fares to win the game.
3. There are no second lives / chances. If lost, you'll have to restart the game.

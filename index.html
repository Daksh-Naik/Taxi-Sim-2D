Taxi Sim 2D

TAXI Sim 2D is a Simulation - Arcade web-browser game, depicting a day in a life of a Taxi Driver. For now, this game is only available for Desktop Browsers (Keyboard mandatory) and for devices with a minimum 1200 PX width resolutions.

As the name suggests, this Game is 2D, you are provided with a Taxi and a plethora of components assigned that will assist you in your journey.

Information

As it's 2D, the realism will curb your expectations. In raw words, you, as a Taxi Driver, have to search for fares, drive upto / near them, pick and drop them at their desired destination. You'll be rewarded with XP. Drop atleast 3 Fares to win the game.

To win, you'll have to keep in mind these notes :

1. Drop atleast 3 Fares to win the game.
2. You have to avoid crashing into Traffic and reaching late.

Let's be practical : In order to win this game, you'll have to search a fare (enclosed in circles) first. Obviously, you can't play football without a ball. Then, drive up your taxi and touch the circle, you'll see information of where the fare desires to go, how far away the destination is, XP and the time the fare has to arrive before. If you're interested, press Y and start your job. That's it.

No, don't assume that the map is large. The map is about 120,000 PX long in length, but only 1,200 PX small in width. Yes, it's a really long strip.

You are free to pick up any Fare you want. The fares are completely randomized. For example, if you're at Point A, you'll find a fare desiring to go to Point B. If you're at Point A still, you'll find a fare desiring to go to Point C.
There are 9 different destination / missions in total and 4 Cities.

Don't worry, there are components assigned to assist you in your journey. Keep an eye on them always.

Tips & Tricks

1. Drive upon the solid line (center line) when searching for a Fare.
2. Take up short distance fares first.
3. Don't give a damn about the timer, you'll have a whole lotta time provided by every Fare.
4. Keep your speed below 25 PX/MS if you're a beginner.
